Why local

Scripture calls the people of God to seek the welfare of the city where He has placed them (Jeremiah 29:7) and to appoint capable, God-fearing leaders over thousands, hundreds, fifties, and tens (Exodus 18:21). The closest government — the council, the school board, the county supervisors — is the one most accountable to faithful citizens who simply show up. Most don't, because it's opaque and time-consuming. This tool removes both excuses.

"Seek the welfare of the city… for in its welfare you will find your welfare." — Jeremiah 29:7

What you get: the next meeting and what's on it, in plain English · who your council members are · the deadlines and steps to comment, speak, or write · a record of what was decided.
